About Us

Renowned and Trusted Facilitator of Trade Finance Services

Euro Exim Bank (EEB), is a resourceful and dynamic global financial institution. Our head office is in St. Lucia, West Indies and the main representative office in London.

We hold a "Class A" international banking license from Financial Services Regulatory Authority (FSRA) of St. Lucia, and an active member of the Caribbean Association of Banks Inc. (CAB) and International Chamber of Commerce (ICC).

With staff, agents, consultants and partners, our team of professionals exceeds 300 people today. We are successfully serving many industries and geographies worldwide with a range of smart trade finance solutions.
